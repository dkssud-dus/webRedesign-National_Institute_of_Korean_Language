The National Institute of Korean Language was established for the purpose of developing the Korean Language, enhancing the public's language competency, and administering research activities.

The Institute conducts research on language policies, manages linguistic rules and standards, publishes Korean Language dictionaries to enhance the public's language use, improves the environment for effective Korean Language communication, strives to enhance Korean Language education quality, and promotes the environment for Korean Sign Language and Korean Braille use.
